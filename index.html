Hostel Description

Girivar Bhawan is a well-maintained boys' hostel located in the heart of the campus. It provides a comfortable living environment for students with modern amenities and facilities. The hostel is equipped with air-conditioned rooms, common areas, and 24/7 security.

Facilities

  • Spacious rooms with study tables and wardrobes
  • High-speed LAN connectivity
  • Common recreational areas including Common room
  • Mess facilities offering nutritious meals
  • Laundry services
  • 24/7 security and CCTV surveillance
  • Badminton Court
